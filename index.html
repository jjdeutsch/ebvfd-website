Welcome

Elgin-Beaverdam Volunteer Fire Hose Co. provides fire protection, rescue services, and community support to the residents of Elgin, Beaverdam, and surrounding areas. Our members are neighbors helping neighbors, trained and ready 24/7.

About Us

We are a rural volunteer fire company committed to protecting life, property, and community through training, teamwork, and service.
